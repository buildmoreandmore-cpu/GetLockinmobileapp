Frequently Asked Questions

What is LockIn?

LockIn is a productivity app that uses tough-love coaching and friction-based design to help you complete focus sessions. Unlike other apps that let you quit easily, LockIn actively challenges you to push through the urge to give up.

How do focus sessions work?

Choose from three session types:

  • Quick Hit: 15 minutes - Perfect for quick tasks
  • Standard: 25 minutes - Classic Pomodoro-style focus
  • Beast Mode: 50+ minutes - For deep work sessions

Once you start, the app enters fullscreen mode and makes it intentionally difficult to quit before completion.

What happens if I try to quit early?

LockIn uses a 3-step exit protocol:

  • Friction Screen: Challenges your impulse with tough messaging
  • Exit Survey: Forces you to state your reason for quitting
  • Hardass Response: Provides coaching based on your excuse

This process creates awareness and makes quitting less automatic.

What are ranks and streaks?

LockIn uses gamification to build discipline:

  • RECRUIT: 0-5 completed sessions
  • SOLDIER: 6-20 completed sessions
  • WARRIOR: 21-50 completed sessions
  • COMMANDER: 51+ completed sessions

Streaks track consecutive days with completed sessions. Quit without a valid reason and you risk losing your streak.

Can I really not quit during a session?

You can always exit the app if there's a genuine emergency. LockIn recognizes legitimate reasons like emergencies, urgent calls, or health issues. However, it will call you out for excuses like "just didn't feel like it."

Safety First: Never use LockIn while driving, operating machinery, or in situations where you need immediate attention.

What data does LockIn collect?

LockIn collects:

  • Focus session data (duration, completion, timestamps)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Analytics data (usage patterns, crash reports)

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Is LockIn available on Android and iOS?

LockIn will be available on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play Store). Check back for launch announcements or sign up for our waitlist.

Is there a free version?

Yes! LockIn offers core focus session functionality for free. Premium features may be available through in-app purchases or subscriptions.

How do I delete my data?

To delete your LockIn data:

  • Open the app and go to Settings
  • Tap "Delete All Data"
  • Confirm deletion

Alternatively, uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data. For analytics data deletion requests, contact us at getlockinmobile@gmail.com.

Technical Support

The app crashed or won't open

Try these steps:

  • Force close the app and restart it
  • Restart your device
  • Check for app updates in the App Store/Play Store
  • Ensure you have the latest OS version
  • If the problem persists, contact support with your device model and OS version

My streak/rank isn't updating

Make sure:

  • You're completing full sessions (not exiting early)
  • The app has permission to send notifications
  • Your device date/time settings are correct

If issues persist, email us with a screenshot of your stats screen.

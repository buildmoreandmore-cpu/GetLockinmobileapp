Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2024

This privacy policy describes how LockIn ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Focus Session Data

We collect and store information about your focus sessions to provide core app functionality and improve your experience:

  • Session duration and completion status
  • Session start and end times
  • Session types (Quick Hit, Standard, Beast Mode)
  • Rank progression and streak data
  • Focus categories selected (Deep Work, Coding, etc.)
  • Exit survey responses (when you quit a session)

Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information to ensure app compatibility and performance:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version number
  • Device language and region settings

Analytics Data

We collect usage analytics to improve app performance and user experience:

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Performance metrics (load times, responsiveness)
  • Aggregated statistics about session completion rates

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide timer functionality, track your progress, and maintain your rank and streak data
  • Personalization: To customize your experience and provide personalized coaching messages
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and identify your peak performance windows
  • Improvement: To fix bugs, improve app performance, and develop new features
  • Communication: To send app updates and respond to support requests

Data Storage and Security

Your focus session data is stored locally on your device and may be backed up to iCloud (iOS) or Google Drive (Android) if you have device backup enabled.

Analytics and crash data is transmitted securely to our analytics providers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for the following purposes:

  • With analytics service providers (e.g., Google Analytics) to improve our services
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • In connection with a business transfer, merger, or acquisition

Third-Party Services

LockIn may integrate with third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Analytics providers process usage data according to their privacy policies
  • App Store/Play Store collect installation and usage data according to Apple/Google privacy policies

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics collection (via app settings)
  • Export your focus session data

Children's Privacy

LockIn is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

International Users

If you are using LockIn from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to request disclosure of data collection and the right to request deletion of personal information.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: getlockinmobile@gmail.com

Response Time: We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.

This privacy policy is compliant with Apple App Store and Google Play Store requirements as of the last updated date.
